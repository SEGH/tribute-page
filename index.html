Elton John & Bernie Taupin

50 Years of Music

Elton John and Bernie Taupin working together in 1971

The power of collaboration can be seen in the 50 year partnership between Elton John and Bernie Taupin.

While Taupin has preferred to stay mostly out of the public eye, John has always acknowledged that without Taupin there very well may not have been Elton John. A chance connection in the late '60s brought the duo together and the magic combination of John's poignant chords and engaging performing along with Taupin's lyrical and relatable storytelling continue to capture fans' hearts. Some of their most-loved hits include:

  • Goodbye Yellow Brick Road
  • Rocket Man
  • Tiny Dancer
  • Candle in the Wind
  • Daniel
  • Your Song
  • I'm Still Standing

And so many more. Read more about their inspiring working relationship on Wikipedia.
